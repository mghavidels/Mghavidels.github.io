The tree city

The Main parts of tree city are divided into 3 main levels.The lower level is allocated to offices, market and some spaces that may need to have lower security and communication with other cities for presenting products.This part is located in the lower place of city because of importance and needs of lights and vitality.the middle level is allocated to the residence and upper is for farming.Tree city includes some details: the levels of the city is clear in this render. a vertical access and farming levels that is deformed to simulate the landscape and hills that is suitable for farming.A spiral road is has been provided that is open access for walking for the people who want to enjoy the views In residence levels hills is designed for sitting like hills in nature for giving nature sense to residents.the levels height up to 10 meters for gaining appropriate sunlight. The whole building of the tree city is divided into 4 parts.the most important part is the levels. They are divided into 3 parts and different height. Another part is vertical main access. The third part is pipes that have two task. The first task is transfer the water and second are structural task .the last is an open space access..
