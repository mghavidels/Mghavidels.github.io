The pipes/LBC-LA International Student Competition, 3rd position/

The main idea for form finding started from grasshopper in rhino as a computational method for finding a form.I used anemone for using a loop that needs start points and end points.the result is joining some separate swept points in 3d dimensional rail and make the form.I always use a concept for form-finding and when the mass created I explore in spaces and different views.Why I used this method for creating form is that this technique gave me a lot of opportunity for having millions type of forms as experiencing forms and space.shadow and light in this form depend on the form of space that can be created million disparate type of space.Use pipes to collect the heat from the solar radiation.As the sun heats the water and glycol mixture in the pipes thermal energy is collected in underground tanks.The heat energy can then be used for multiple purposes including; heating the building at night by recirculating the stored hot water, cooling the building during the day using absorption chillers, or even for generating electricity using the thermal gradients to propel turbines..
